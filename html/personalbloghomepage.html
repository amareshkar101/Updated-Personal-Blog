A little about me

My name is Brooke and I am a Front-End Development student in Seattle, WA. My passions include traveling, playing violin, and programming. I started learning how to program when the pandemic struck, mainly as a brief distraction I could implement into my day when daily life became a little bit more overwhelming than normal. Since then, I have branched out into JavaScript, HMTL, and CSS. I love all of the creative possibilities there are with front-end, and I'm excited to show you my future projects!









My Home

I was born and raised in the beautiful Pacific NorthWest. Growing up just hours South of Seattle, I was never far away from the beach, mountains, or desert. Some of my favorite locations in the PNW are Canon Beach, Alki Beach, and The Paradise Hiking Trails near Mt. Rainer.
